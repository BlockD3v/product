## The Three Pillars

### Pillar 1: Asset-Aware Context Switching
When you select gold, the interface shows supply/demand data, CME market hours, and geopolitical news. When you select ETH, it shows funding rates, OI, and liquidation levels. Same terminal, different context.

**Why it matters**: Professional platforms (IBKR, thinkorswim, Bloomberg) all do this. No HL frontend does.

### Pillar 2: Fragmentation Resolution
TSLA is listed by TradeXYZ (USDC), Felix (USDH), and Ventuals (USDH). HypeTerminal shows ONE "TSLA" and routes to the best price/liquidity. Like 1inch for HL's fragmented HIP-3 markets.

**Why it matters**: Confirmed community confusion ("two versions of Tesla is confusing"). First to solve this wins.

### Pillar 3: Progressive Simplification
Simple mode for beginners (market orders, no leverage, clean interface). Pro mode for power users (full order book, all order types, custom layouts). Auto-detect and suggest based on trading history.

**Why it matters**: HL is "dead simple for power users, painful for beginners." We serve both.

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Commodity Traders (Immediate Opportunity)

**Why start here**:
- 40% of HL volume, growing fastest
- Traditional commodity traders migrating (JPMorgan confirmed)
- NO existing frontend targets this segment
- These traders are underserved by crypto-first UIs
- Weekend trading during geopolitical events = high urgency, high volume

**What to ship**:
- Commodity-optimized layout (supply/demand panel, market hours, geopolitical news)
- Oil, gold, silver focus (top 3 commodity volumes)
- CME open/closed indicator
- Weekend trading context ("trade oil while CME sleeps")

**Distribution**:
- Target commodity trading communities (Fintwit, commodity Discord/Telegram)
- Content: "Why oil traders are moving to Hyperliquid — and how to trade like a pro"
- Referral program: builder code revenue share with referrers

### Phase 2: Multi-Asset Dashboard

**What to ship**:
- Cross-asset portfolio view (crypto + commodities + equities in one dashboard)
- Correlation matrix between positions
- Exposure breakdown by asset class
- Unified PnL across everything

### Phase 3: Equity Traders + Fragmentation Resolution

**What to ship**:
- Equity-optimized layout (fundamentals, earnings calendar, sector heatmap)
- HIP-3 builder aggregation (best price routing across duplicate markets)
- "This is a perpetual contract" disclaimers
- Traditional market hours context

### Phase 4: Social & Advanced Features

**What to ship**:
- Risk-adjusted leaderboards
- Copy trading with risk limits
- AI co-pilot (natural language orders)
- Mobile PWA with push notifications
