# USDH is a Trojan horse

## Angle

USDH isn't a stablecoin — it's a fee-rebate vehicle disguised as one. The economics are designed to migrate the entire perps order book off USDC. Native Markets won the bid in Sept 2025 with reserves at BlackRock and JPM/Fireblocks custody. Few writers have noticed that the rebate stack + Assistance Fund flywheel is *deliberately* built to make USDC use economically irrational on Hyperliquid.

## Why now

- USDH live since Sept 24, 2025; integration with Felix, Ventuals, HIP-3 markets ongoing
- Rebate program is in production but underanalyzed
- USDH adoption curve = leading indicator for HYPE buyback acceleration

## Key evidence — the trap

- USDH-quoted markets: **20% lower taker fees, +50% maker rebates, +20% volume tier credit**
- 50% of USDH revenue → Assistance Fund (= HYPE buybacks)
- Reserves: US Treasuries + cash, BlackRock managed, JPM custody, Superstate on-chain
- Margin asset on HIP-3 (Felix, Ventuals, more)

## Why it works (the flywheel)

1. Trader switches USDC → USDH for cheaper fees
2. USDH float grows → Treasury yield grows
3. 50% of yield → buys HYPE → burns
4. HYPE price up → Assistance Fund worth more → cushions volatility → more trader confidence
5. Loop
